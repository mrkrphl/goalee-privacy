Introduction

Welcome to Goalee ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data.

Data We Collect

  • Your Name: The name you provide during onboarding
  • Selected Categories: Life areas you track (Health, Focus, Work, Social, Recovery)
  • Save Data: Habit tracking data including titles, categories, completion status, and timestamps
  • Usage Statistics: Progress tracking, completion rates, and streak information

How We Store Your Data

All data is stored locally on your device. Goalee uses your device's local storage (AsyncStorage and SecureStore). We do not transmit your personal data to any remote servers or cloud services.

How We Use Your Data

  • Display your personalized habit tracking dashboard
  • Calculate and show your progress, streaks, and statistics
  • Provide insights about your habit completion patterns
  • Remember your preferences and settings

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties. Since all data is stored locally and never transmitted to our servers, there is no data sharing with external parties, advertisers, or analytics services.

Data Retention and Deletion

  • Delete All Data: Settings → Delete All Data
  • Uninstalling the App: Removes all locally stored data
  • No Recovery: Once deleted, data cannot be recovered (no external backups)

Children's Privacy

Goalee is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, security depends on your device's security measures (passcode, biometric authentication, device encryption).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Check the "Last updated" date at the top. We encourage periodic review.
